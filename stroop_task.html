Stroop Task Experiment

A short cognitive experiment studying how the brain handles conflicting information. You'll complete 3 rounds of 30 trials each. Read the instructions at the start of each round carefully.

What to expect
• 3 rounds × 30 trials = 90 total responses
• Each round has different instructions — read them carefully
• Your response time and accuracy are recorded anonymously
• Takes approximately 5–10 minutes to complete
